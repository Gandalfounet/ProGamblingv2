FAQ

How to enter Jackpot?

  1. Sign in through Steam
  2. Put an actual Trade Offer link into your settings
  3. Make your inventory public in the Steam Profile
  4. Click deposit button and validate your trade offer

What is the maximum item amount?

Maximum is {{config_site.max_item}} items per round. You can deposit multiple times until the amount is less than {{config_site.max_item}} items.

What is the minimum deposit?

Minimum deposit is {{config_site.min_deposit}}$! All items are accepted. There's no maximum!

How long is one round?

A round lasts 60 seconds until 2 players joined

How to get the winning?

At the end of a round, we send the trade offer to the winner. It can take up to 5 minutes depending of the steam network.

I didn't receive my winnings?
